About Us Here at The Shelter Stop

The Shelter Stop was created to increase views of adoptable pets, in order to maximize the rehoming rates of those friends in need.
When searching for a new pet, it's important to find your perfect match, but what if that perfect match isn't showing on one site? Or in one shelter?
That's where The Shelter Stop comes in.
By pulling adoptable pets from multiple locations, we provide a one stop shop that will help ensure no friend in need is overlooked. We hope we can help you find your furever friend, wherever they may be.
